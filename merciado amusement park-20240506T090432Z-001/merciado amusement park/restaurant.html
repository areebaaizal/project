SEABREEZE GRILL

Located in the Midway, the Seabreeze Grill offers a variety of food choices everyone will love. You can dine a la cart or choose a combo meal to satisfy your taste buds. Covered picnic table seating is available.

CALIFORNIA GRILL

Located in the Water Park, the California Grill is the perfect place to grab a meal after enjoying all the slides! Pick up a cheeseburger or tasty snack, the grill has it all. Right across from the grill, picnic seating is available.
