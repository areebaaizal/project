    ABOUT US

    Merciado Amusement park is a nationwide brand, which comes under the umbrella of Pioneer Amusement Services (Pvt.) Ltd that is a subsidiary of the “Dolmen Group”. We come with a vision to develop International Standard Amusement landscapes in Pakistan. Over the years,Merciado Amusement park has expanded its operations within the major metropolitan cities of Pakistan including Karachi & Hyderabad and we intend to continue the growth at the same pace in the near future.
    The Merciado Amusement park arcade has been built both indoors and outdoors to provide an environment of equal thrill for fun seekers. The arcade has a wide range of rides including a Drop Tower, Dodgem Cars, a 7-D Cinema, plus many other exciting rides. Merciado Amusement park is the perfect place for a fun day out! There are plenty of rides to keep everyone entertained, from the fast and thrilling to the gentle and nostalgic. There’s also a great selection of food and drink outlets, so you can refuel after a busy day of exploring the park. And if you’re looking for something extra special, Chunky Monkey Park regularly hosts events and parties – perfect for birthday celebrations or simply a fun night out! So whether you’re young or young at heart, there’s something for everyone at DREAM WEAVER AMUSEMENT PARK.
